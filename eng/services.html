CONCRETE WORKS

The company carries out the installation of structures, constructions, their elements of any complexity using concrete pouring method, strictly adhering to all technologies. The high qualification of our company's engineers allows for the calculation of any loads during concreting of various thicknesses. Our company guarantees qualified execution and control of concrete works, ensuring maximum reliability, safety, and durability of finished structures. We offer the best conditions for our clients.

Concrete work technology is conventionally divided into several key stages:

  • Transportation of basic materials to the site;
  • Preparatory work;
  • Formwork creation;
  • Preparation of concrete mix;
  • Pouring;
  • Subsequent processing and formwork removal.

The preparatory stage includes the selection of suitable concrete mix (chosen according to the type of future structure), type, and material of formwork. Almost the entire subsequent concreting process depends on these components. Proper preparation, as well as adherence to the appropriate mix delivery-reception technology, will ensure uniform filling of the entire formwork area and timely setting of the mix.

INSULATION WORKS FOR BUILDING AND STRUCTURE CONSTRUCTIONS, PIPELINES, INDUSTRIAL EQUIPMENT

The company specializes and has extensive experience in conducting comprehensive insulation works in the following areas:

  • Insulation of building structures, including insulation of floors, ceilings, pitched and flat roofs, attic spaces, and the arrangement of facade systems. These works aim to save heat and reduce heating costs.
  • Insulation of pipelines, aimed at corrosion protection and maintaining a stable temperature inside the pipes.
  • Insulation of equipment, necessary for protection against overheating and reducing vibration loads by lining the housings with insulation materials.

Various types of materials are used in the insulation works:

  • Insulation boards based on mineral and organic materials. These can be basalt and glass wool insulation, as well as wood fiber boards.
  • Insulation mats, which are flexible and necessary for insulating structures of irregular and round shapes. Mats are also produced based on mineral and organic materials.
  • Panels made of synthetic materials such as polyethylene and polystyrene foam. They are often used for insulating non-load-bearing structures due to their lightweight.
  • Insulation compounds used for treating metal structures such as pipelines and equipment casings.

We carry out projects through the entire cycle – from developing insulation systems and estimating the required materials to manufacturing and commissioning of insulated objects.

Our solutions enable cost reduction for projects and improve the operational characteristics of equipment.
